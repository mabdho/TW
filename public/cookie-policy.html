Cookie Policy

Last Updated: July 13, 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

TravelWanders uses cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Provide personalized travel recommendations
  • Analyze website usage and performance
  • Improve user experience and functionality
  • Ensure website security

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Required for basic website functionality Session
Analytics Cookies Help us understand website usage Up to 2 years
Functional Cookies Remember your preferences Up to 1 year
Marketing Cookies Deliver relevant advertisements Up to 1 year

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may also use third-party cookies from trusted partners to:

  • Provide analytics and insights (Google Analytics)
  • Enable social media integration
  • Deliver targeted advertising
  • Improve website performance

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Opt-Out Tools

You can opt out of targeted advertising cookies through:

  • Google Ads Settings
  • Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page
  • Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out page

6. Impact of Disabling Cookies

Please note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality:

  • Some features may not work properly
  • Personalization may be limited
  • You may need to re-enter information
  • Performance may be reduced

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page.
